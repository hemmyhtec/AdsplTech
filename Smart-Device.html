Building Scalable IoT Products (Smart device)

ADSPL is bringing a series of “Intelli+Save” products for your Smart Home which will be truly a steppingstone for anyone who wants to enjoy the experience of life in the future.
Intelli+Save – Nexgen

Intelli+Save devices are everyday objects that are made intelligent with advanced computing, including artificial intelligence and machine learning, and connected to the Internet of Things (IoT).

Intelli+Save can operate at the edge of the network or at very small endpoints, and while small, they are powerful enough to process command without logging into the cloud.

All Intelligent devices combined is something which can make your live stylish, wonderful and comfortable. Our devices help improve efficiency and streamline operations.
