Intelli+save SENSORS

Temperature Sensors

Temperature sensors are capable of detecting any fluctuations of temperature in their surroundings.
The information from these temperature sensors are used by our Intelli+save connectors for regulation of the temperature within the rooms to a desired level, to perform certain actions such as turning on the fans and air conditioners, rolling down the curtains etc. based on the user’s request.

Humidity Sensors

Humidity sensors are a great way to keep in check the humidity levels. The ideal humid level within homes should range between 30 percent and 50 percent.
If the moisture level goes below or above this range, it leads to allergy, dryness of the skin or at higher levels a feeling of heaviness and air becomes suffocating. Our humidity sensors detect any change in the moisture level & provide recommendation in maintaining the air quality and alert you about presence of allergens, mold growth etc.

Optic/ Light Sensors

Optic sensors detect the ambient light levels. These sensors are useful in measuring the external light levels and accordingly switching on/off of the lights to conserve energy.
Our sensors are used for controlling all the lighting installations within your homes – turn them on/off or change their brightness as and when required.

Fire/Smoke sensors

We take safety of people at the highest priority, when a fire breaks out, the timing of alert is very crucial.
It is here that importance of fire/ smoke sensors comes into light. Our sensors come with CO (carbon monoxide) detector that alert you whenever there is unusually high level of CO inside the building.

Camera / Proximity/Motion Sensors

The Camera and motion sensors are crucial for ensuring safety of your home and property especially when you are not present at your homes.
These IoT sensors can alert you of any suspicious activity inside or around your home. These sensors sense any motion or vibration and can respond in the form of alert to you
